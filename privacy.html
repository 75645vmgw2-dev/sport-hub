Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Welcome to Kazmo ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address, name, and profile data when you create an account.
  • Usage data: Sports preferences, favorite teams, and app interactions.
  • Device information: Push notification tokens, device type (iOS/Android), and language settings.
  • Authentication data: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive basic profile information from those services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide live sports scores, predictions, and personalized content.
  • To send push notifications about matches and sports events you follow.
  • To improve the app and develop new features.
  • To communicate with you about updates and changes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (a cloud database provider). We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Push Notifications

We use Expo's push notification service to send alerts. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or within the app.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • API-Sports — live sports data
  • Anthropic Claude — AI-powered analysis
  • Expo — push notifications
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In — authentication

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Kazmo is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at jack.melki@mac.com to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
